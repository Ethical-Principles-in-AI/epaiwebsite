UofT Ethical Principles in AI Club

The Ethical Principles in Artificial Intelligence Team (EPAI) is committed to educating and taking action on ethical concerns within artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data science. Our mission is to make the ethics in AI more accessible through educational workshops and practical projects. We welcome all students from all backgrounds of knowledge and experience.
